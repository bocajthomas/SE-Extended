**1. Some Snapchat+ Features Don't Work**

**2. I Have A Feature Request**

**3. Will Features Get You Banned**
- Theres a very high chance you will get banned, So please use this module at your own risk, Features with a higher risk is marked with `⚠️ This feature may cause bans`

**5. I have a bug / Issue**
